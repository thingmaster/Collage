How to Use

1. Load/drop photos, set paper size, click Generate

2. Click a cell to select; drag to pan image

3. Scroll wheel on selected cell to zoom

4. Click Resize Cells or double-click margin

5. In resize mode, drag any edge to resize

6. Shift+drag to swap images between cells

7. Drop a photo on a cell to replace it

8. Ctrl+V to paste overlay from clipboard

9. Click Export JPEG to save
